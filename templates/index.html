How does Artificial Intelligence analyze images?

An AI virtual dermatologist analyzes skin images using deep learning models trained on labeled datasets of skin diseases. The images are preprocessed and fed into models like CNNs or Vision Transformers, which identify patterns and classify conditions based on learned features. The system is trained and tested to ensure accuracy using medical datasets.

When a user uploads a skin image, the AI processes it and predicts the most likely condition, assigning confidence scores. It may highlight affected areas and suggest treatments or refer the user to a dermatologist for further evaluation.

To ensure safety and reliability, the system follows medical regulations like HIPAA and GDPR. It continuously improves by learning from new data and is designed to assist, not replace, human dermatologists for better skin health management...
